ABOUT US

Crafting Quality.
Building Trust.

Founded with a clear purpose — to revolutionise India's construction and interior industry by replacing traditional wood with superior Wood Polymer Composite solutions. VNEX WPC has spent over a decade engineering products that outlast, outperform, and out-beautify conventional alternatives.

Our doors, frames, and sheets are crafted using cutting-edge German extrusion technology, blended from high-grade PVC resin and natural wood fiber. Every product undergoes rigorous quality checks before leaving our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility.
