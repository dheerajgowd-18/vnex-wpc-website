PRODUCT ADVANTAGES

Why Our Frames Outperform Traditional Wood

🚫

Zero Warping, Ever

WPC's cellular structure doesn't absorb moisture — eliminating the warping, bowing, and swelling that plagues timber frames over time.

🏗️

Structural Rigidity

High compressive strength handles the full weight and daily operational stress of heavy doors without sagging or deforming over the years.

🌊

Coastal & Wet Area Ready

Performs flawlessly in coastal properties, bathrooms, kitchens, and basements where traditional wood frames fail within a few years.

⚡

Quick Installation

Pre-mitered at corners with precision CNC cutting — reduces installation time by up to 40% compared to custom-cut timber frames on-site.

🐜

Pest-Proof Guarantee

No organic material in the composition means no termites, no borers, and no white ants can ever attack or compromise the frame's integrity.

🔧

Workability

Can be cut, drilled, and shaped with standard woodworking tools — making field adjustments and custom fitting simple and efficient.
