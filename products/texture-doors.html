PRODUCT ADVANTAGES

What Makes Our Texture Doors Special

🌿

Authentic Emboss Depth

Our embossing process creates multi-level grain patterns that mirror real wood cross-sections — a visual and tactile authenticity that flat-printed doors cannot match.

🎨

Wide Colour Range

20+ colour shades per texture family — from light ash and honey oak to rich espresso walnut and charcoal wenge — for every interior style.

🛡️

Scratch & Dent Resistant

The hardened WPC surface resists everyday impacts, scratches, and scuffs that would damage traditional wood or hollow-core alternatives.

🔇

Sound Dampening

Dense composite core provides natural acoustic insulation, reducing sound transmission between rooms for improved privacy.

🌡️

All-Climate Performance

From Kerala's humidity to Rajasthan's dry heat, VNEX Texture Doors maintain their shape, colour, and integrity in any Indian climate zone.

🔧

Easy Hardware Fitment

The dense WPC core holds door hinges, locks, and handles securely without splitting — no need for special screws or anchors.
