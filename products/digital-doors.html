What Makes Our Digital Doors Special

🎨

HD Digital Printing

Industrial-grade UV inkjet printing delivers colours and textures that rival actual wood, marble, and stone with stunning visual depth.

☀️

UV-Stable Colours

Special UV-resistant inks ensure that colours and designs remain vibrant even with years of sunlight exposure — guaranteed fade-free.

💧

Fully Waterproof

The composite WPC core doesn't absorb moisture. It's fully suitable for bathrooms, utility areas, and any high-humidity environment.

🔒

Solid Core Strength

High-density WPC core provides superior structural rigidity compared to hollow-core doors — adding security, soundproofing, and longevity.

♻️

Eco-Friendly Materials

Manufactured using recycled wood fiber and reclaimed PVC, VNEX Digital Doors significantly reduce deforestation and environmental impact.

🎯

Custom Artwork Ready

Submit your own artwork, brand pattern, or custom design — our print team can produce fully bespoke doors for premium hospitality projects.
