Whether you're fabricating bespoke furniture, building modular cabinetry, laying wall panels, or creating commercial partitions, VNEX WPC Sheets deliver the performance and finish that interior professionals demand.

PRODUCT ADVANTAGES

Why Interior Professionals Choose VNEX Sheets

🔩

Superior Machinability

Cuts cleanly with circular saws, routers, and CNC machines — no splintering or chipping on edges. Perfect for precision cabinetry work.

💧

No Delamination

Unlike MDF or plywood, VNEX WPC Sheets won't swell and delaminate when exposed to water leaks or sustained humidity — laminate stays bonded.

⚖️

Dimensional Consistency

Every sheet maintains precise thickness and density within ±0.3mm — critical for furniture fabrication and quality modular systems.

🏋️

High Load Capacity

Excellent flexural strength allows VNEX sheets to support heavy kitchen countertops, wardrobe structures, and commercial shelving systems.

🎨

Universal Adhesion

Compatible with all standard laminates, veneers, PU paints, and contact adhesives — no special primers required for finishing.

♻️

Eco-Responsible

Manufactured from recycled wood fiber — helping reduce pressure on forests while giving designers a superior material to work with.
