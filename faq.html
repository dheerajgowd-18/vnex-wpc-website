Common Questions About VNEX WPC

We've compiled the most frequently asked questions from our customers, architects, and contractors. Don't see your question? Contact us directly.

VNEX WPC products are 100% waterproof — not merely water-resistant. The composite's cellular structure contains no natural fibers that can absorb water. You can submerge our products in water indefinitely and they will not swell, delaminate, rot, or lose structural integrity. This makes them suitable for bathrooms, kitchens, basements, and even outdoor-adjacent applications in India's monsoon climate.
Absolutely not. Termites, borers, and white ants feed on cellulose found in organic wood. VNEX WPC is manufactured from PVC polymer and processed wood fiber — the fiber is fully encapsulated within the PVC matrix and is completely inaccessible to insects. No termite treatment, anti-pest spray, or fumigation is ever required. This is one of the most significant lifetime cost savings compared to traditional wood.
Yes — we offer full customization across all product lines. For doors, we can produce any dimension within our manufacturing capacity, in any standard or custom design, and in multiple thickness options. For sheets, we offer a wide range of thicknesses from 3mm to 25mm, and both standard and custom sheet sizes. Please contact our sales team with your project requirements and our technical team will provide a tailored solution.
Pricing varies based on product type, size, thickness, design complexity, and order volume. We offer competitive pricing for individual, contractor, and bulk/project orders. For bulk orders (10+ doors or 50+ sheets), significant volume discounts are available. Please contact us via the quote form on our Contact page and our team will provide a detailed, project-specific quotation within 24 working hours.
VNEX WPC doors are installed using standard carpentry methods and tools — the same process used for traditional wooden doors. Standard hardware (hinges, handles, locks, door closers) can be fitted directly into the WPC core without any special anchors or inserts. Our frames are pre-mitered and pre-drilled for faster on-site installation. We recommend professional installation to ensure optimal performance and warranty coverage.
Yes — sustainability is central to our manufacturing philosophy. VNEX WPC is made using recycled wood fiber sourced from industrial wood waste and reclaimed PVC material. Our products directly substitute for natural timber, helping reduce deforestation. Additionally, because WPC products last significantly longer than wood alternatives, they reduce the frequency of replacement — further lowering long-term environmental impact.
All VNEX WPC products carry a 10-year structural warranty covering manufacturing defects, delamination, and material failures under normal use conditions. Our warranty covers structural performance but does not cover physical damage caused by misuse, improper installation, or accidents. We encourage all buyers to register their purchase with us for streamlined warranty service — our team typically responds to warranty claims within 48 hours.
Virtually none. This is one of WPC's greatest advantages over traditional wood. You will never need to sand, stain, varnish, paint, or apply anti-pest treatments. For routine cleaning, simply wipe the surface with a damp cloth or a mild household cleaner. For our digital print doors, avoid abrasive cleaners or scouring pads. For sheets, the laminated surface is maintained exactly as you would any laminate furniture — easy wipe-clean care.

Bulk orders for large projects may require 3–4 weeks depending on quantity and specifications. Yes. Our WPC compound includes fire-retardant additives as part of the standard formulation. VNEX WPC products are classified as self-extinguishing — meaning they do not continue to burn after the ignition source is removed. They are significantly safer than traditional wood in fire scenarios. For special applications requiring certified fire ratings (like commercial buildings), please discuss your specific requirements with our technical team who can recommend the appropriate product grade.
VNEX WPC Sheets are available in 3mm, 6mm, 9mm, 12mm, 18mm, and 25mm standard thicknesses. For furniture and cabinet box work, 12mm and 18mm are most commonly used. For wall paneling and decorative applications, 6mm and 9mm are popular. For structural or load-bearing applications, 18mm and 25mm are recommended. For thicknesses outside this range or for custom-engineered applications, please contact our technical sales team.
